
# Datasets and Information Sources for PCMC Water and Energy Analytics

This document contains information about all the datasets and sources used in the PCMC Smart City analytics portal.

## Official PCMC Documents

1. PCMC Green City Action Plan
   - Source: PCMC Official Website
   - URL: https://www.pcmcindia.gov.in/marathi/pdf/Green-City-Action-Plan.pdf
   - Description: Contains comprehensive information about PCMC's green initiatives, sustainability goals, and environmental plans.
   - Data Used: Environmental metrics, green cover statistics, sustainability targets

2. Water Sustainability Assessment of Pune by TERI
   - Source: The Energy and Resources Institute
   - URL: https://www.teriin.org/sites/default/files/2021-06/Water_Sustainability_Assessment_%20of_Pune.pdf
   - Description: Detailed assessment of water resources, usage patterns, and sustainability initiatives in the Pune metropolitan area, including PCMC.
   - Data Used: Water consumption patterns, groundwater levels, sustainability metrics

3. PCMC's Water Conservation Using Data Analytics
   - Source: Economic Times CIO
   - URL: https://cio.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/business-analytics/heres-how-punes-pcmc-is-saving-31000-million-litres-of-water-using-data-and-analytics/85582938
   - Description: Case study on how PCMC used data analytics to conserve water resources.
   - Data Used: Water conservation metrics, leak detection statistics, success rates

4. PCMC Comprehensive Environmental Pollution Index (CEPI) Report
   - Source: Maharashtra Pollution Control Board
   - URL: https://mpcb.gov.in/sites/default/files/inline-files/8_MPCB_CEPI_Report_Pimpri_Chinchwad_March_2024.pdf
   - Description: Report on environmental pollution metrics in the PCMC area.
   - Data Used: Air quality indices, water pollution metrics, industrial emissions data

## Additional Data Sources

5. Electricity Consumption Dataset
   - Source: GitHub (processed from MSEDCL data)
   - URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/aniketmahajan-29/Electricity-Consumption-EDA-Analysis/main/Dataset.csv
   - Description: Multi-year electricity consumption data for PCMC and surrounding areas
   - Data Used: Monthly electricity consumption, per capita usage, peak demand

6. Water Sustainability Dataset
   - Source: GitHub
   - URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lovable-data/pcmc-data/main/water_sustainability.csv
   - Description: Historical data on water demand, supply, and related metrics
   - Data Used: Water demand trends, supply capacity, deficit analysis

7. PCMC Green City Initiative Dataset
   - Source: GitHub
   - URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lovable-data/pcmc-data/main/pcmc_green_city.csv
   - Description: Dataset tracking progress on PCMC's green city initiatives
   - Data Used: Energy consumption, renewable percentage, water recycling metrics
